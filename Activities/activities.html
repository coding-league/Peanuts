MOBILE APPS

It’s the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown

Everyone’s favorite Halloween special is re-imagined in this interactive storybook. Choose from various costume combinations to dress up and trick-or-treat with the gang, carve your own pumpkin, play a little ditty on Schroeder’s piano and bob for apples with Snoopy — just watch out for dog germs, blech!

A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving

Join Charlie Brown and the Peanuts gang around the table for a very unique Thanksgiving dinner as the classic comes to life in this interactive storybook, A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving will leave your senses hungry for a second helping.

A Charlie Brown Christmas

Help Charlie Brown, Snoopy, and the entire Peanuts gang as they struggle to find the true meaning of Christmas in this interactive storybook complete with original dialogue from the 1965 classic, digitally remastered illustrations, animation, and music.

Charlie Brown’s All-Stars!

Charlie Brown’s All-Stars! springs to life in this charming interactive storybook. Peanuts fans will delight in sharing this classic story of team spirit and friendship for a whole new generation of underdogs!

Snoopy Puzzle Journey

Join Snoopy on an around-the-world sightseeing tour. As you tap matching blocks to remove them from the board, you clear the way for Snoopy’s next adventure.

How to Draw Marcie

Help Snoopy search for his sister Belle and the secret candy drops. Don’t be surprised if you discover other members of the Peanuts Gang along the way! (Japan only)

Snoopy Life

A storm has hit the Peanuts Gang’s town. Find the missing objects and help rebuild the town with the help of Snoopy and the entire Peanuts crew. (Japan only)

Snoopy's Town Tale

Create your own town for the Peanuts characters, complete with doghouse and baseball field, in this engaging world-building simulation game.
